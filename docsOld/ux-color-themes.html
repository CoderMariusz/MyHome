üìù Color Usage Guidelines

  • Primary Brand Color: Main buttons, links, key CTAs, selected states
  • Secondary/Action Color: Secondary actions, success states, purchased items
  • Warm Accent: Notifications, highlights, urgent actions
  • Background Colors: App background (lightest), cards (middle), borders (darkest)
  • Text Hierarchy: Headings (primary text), body (secondary text), hints (disabled text)
  • Success Green: Completed items, positive feedback, confirmations
  • Warning Amber: Alerts, reminders, attention needed
  • Error Red: Validation errors, destructive actions, critical issues
  • Accessibility: All color combinations meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA (4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum)
  • Recommendation: Sky & Earth theme best balances trust (blue), family warmth (green), accessibility, and scalability
